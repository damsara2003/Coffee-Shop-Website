What Makes Our Coffee Special?

Crema is a brownish foam that forms on the top of freshly made espresso. The presence of Crema represents a good quality and fresh coffee.

I wanted to take this time to let you know how grateful we are to have you choosing us for your coffee needs. Quality coffee is a passion for me and I am so thankful for your patronage which allows me to do this. I wanted to share a little about our coffees, and why I think they are so special.

First of all, we buy our coffee from only the most reputable farms and mills in Kona. Being a teacher in Kona for three decades, I taught some of the farmers and some of their parents too - which goes a long way.
